Privacy Policy

DueMate is designed to work locally on your iPhone. The app does not require an account and does not operate a server.

Last updated: May 20, 2026

English

Item Data

Names, categories, quantities, expiry dates, reminder choices, and notes are stored locally on your device using Apple system storage. DueMate does not collect or upload this data.

Network

DueMate does not make network requests for its core features. There is no login, analytics SDK, advertising SDK, or third-party tracking SDK.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, DueMate schedules local expiry reminders on your device. Notification permission can be changed anytime in iOS Settings.

Export

CSV export files are generated locally. Data leaves the app only if you choose a destination in the iOS share sheet.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at jay212315@gmail.com.

简体中文

隐私政策

期限管家被设计为在你的 iPhone 本地运行。App 不需要账号，也不运营服务器。

物品数据

名称、分类、数量、到期日期、提醒选择和备注会使用 Apple 系统存储保存在你的设备本地。期限管家不会收集或上传这些数据。

网络

期限管家的核心功能不发起网络请求。没有登录、分析 SDK、广告 SDK 或第三方追踪 SDK。

通知

如果你开启通知，期限管家会在你的设备上安排本地到期提醒。你可以随时在 iOS 设置中修改通知权限。

导出

CSV 导出文件在本地生成。只有当你在 iOS 分享面板中选择目标时，数据才会离开 App。

联系方式

如果你对本隐私政策有任何问题，请通过 jay212315@gmail.com 联系我们。
